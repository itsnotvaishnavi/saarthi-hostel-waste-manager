About Our Initiative at SRM

We at SRM Institute of Science and Technology are committed to promoting sustainable waste management in hostels. By recycling waste, you help keep the campus clean while earning rewards!

Why Recycle?

  • Reduce landfill waste and pollution.

  • Promote a cleaner and greener environment.

Exciting Rewards

Discount Coupons

Get discounts at your favorite stores.

Gift Vouchers

Earn vouchers for top brands.

Free Meals

Enjoy free meals at the canteen.

How It Works

  1. Collect waste and segregate it properly.
  2. Bring the waste to our collection points across SRM hostels.
  3. Earn rewards based on the type and quantity of waste.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of waste is accepted?

We accept plastic, paper, metal, and organic waste.

How are rewards calculated?

Rewards are based on the type and quantity of waste collected from SRM hostels.
